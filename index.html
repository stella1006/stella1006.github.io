Research

Efficient Similarity Search with Proximity Graph

Proximity Graph, Similarity Search

The first part is the construction of nearest neighbor graph. I analyzed a wealth of literature regarding including KNN-Graph, NS-graph, NN-descent, HNSW and tried to improve NN-descent by using the idea of segregation and reconstruction.

The second part is to improve the efficiency of searching by increasing the connectivity of the nearest neighbor graph. I applied variously related algorithms including Iterative Quantization, Product Quantization, and Inverted Multi-Index.

June 2018 - Present

Developing Fake News Detection Algorithm

Embedding Text, Supervised Learning, Neural network

This is a group project, to build an end to end Machine learning pipeline that will ingest raw text data, process the raw text data into paragraph vectors and apply trained supervised learning classifiers to the paragraph vectors to label the original text as fake or not fake.

  • I compared different methods for word embedding applications. I also tried neural embedding implementations like Gensim on both for word vectorization and for paragraph vectorization and also used Tensorflow hub library to embed text.
  • I used standard industry classifiers to classify fake or fake news including linear regression, logistics regression, random trees, decision trees, XGB boost, Support Vector Machines.
